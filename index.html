Our Story

Crafting Dreams in
Gold & Diamonds

"Where every jewel carries the soul of Indian craftsmanship."

Established over three decades ago, Abhushan Jewellers has been a trusted name for families seeking heirloom-quality jewelry in Mumbai. We blend age-old Indian artisanal traditions with contemporary design sensibilities.

From intricate Kundan bridal sets to sparkling solitaire diamonds, every piece is curated with uncompromising standards of purity, craftsmanship, and beauty. Our BIS hallmarked gold and certified diamonds ensure complete peace of mind.
